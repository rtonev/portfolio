Michigan Information Interaction Lab
  • Date: September 2016 - Present
  • Role: UX Researcher/Designer
  • Work Setting: Lab team of 9

We investigate new methods, tools and technologies that enable users to interact with information in more natural and powerful ways, and also make it easier for designers to create more usable and effective interfaces. Ultimately, our research will allow more users to become designers.

Lab Birds-Eye View

Currently, I am designing ways of which the lab devices could be controlled by users. As one of the components, I am designing a brids-eye view interface that allows a lab user to move the content shown on one screen to another.
