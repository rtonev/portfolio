Summary

For my capstone class project, I designed an online learning platform that aims to improve student engagement and reduce isolation in online courses. The platform facilitates social interactions and peer learning experiences for students.

Stakeholder Map

In order to better understand the problem, I created a stakeholder map. The map made me consider all sides of education in general and helped me think of the various possible issues I could explore within online education.


Love and Breakup Letters

In oder to further try to clarify the problem that I am solving and the audience that I am solving for, I wrote love and breakup letters to my product. More details about this design technique can be found ------here------.


Background Research

In order to explore the problem space, I conducted background research, which included literature review in the following domains:

  • Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) and eLearning
  • Online peer collaboration and learning
  • Learning through video
  • Individual-Group interactions in online communities
  • Computer supported collaborative learning
  • Dropout rates in MOOCs
  • Alienation in online courses
